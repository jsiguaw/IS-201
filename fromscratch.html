Why I Like Climbing

Climbing is a great combination of strength, problem solving, and endurance. I like that every route feels like a puzzle and that progress comes from patience and consistency.

Types of Climbing

  1. Bouldering
    • Short routes
    • No ropes
    • Focuses on power and technique
  2. Top Rope
    • Great for beginners
    • Uses a rope already anchored above
    • Helps build confidence
  3. Lead Climbing
    • More advanced
    • Requires clipping into protection while climbing
    • Builds mental toughness
